Fried Rice

Description

This fried rice recipe only takes 15 minutes to cook and tastes just like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant. Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need. Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Ingredients
  • 400gr of Rice
  • Large onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 200 gr of peas
  • 2 eggs
  • 100gr of ham
  • 2tbs ketjap manis
Instructions
  1. Cook rice
  2. Dice onion and garlic. Cook until glaze
  3. Throw everyting together in wokpan
  4. Add ketjap and sambal
  5. Stirfry for 3 mins
